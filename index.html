About Red Dead Redemption 2 Mobile

Step into the boots of Arthur Morgan in this groundbreaking mobile version of the critically acclaimed Western epic. Experience the vast American frontier like never before, with stunning mobile-optimized graphics and intuitive touch controls.

Features

• Immersive open-world gameplay optimized for mobile

• Stunning graphics with dynamic weather and day/night cycle

• Deep story missions and side activities

• Customizable controls and auto-aim options

• Cloud save support across devices

System Requirements

Android:

• Android 10.0 or higher

• 6GB RAM minimum

• 8GB free storage space

iOS:

• iOS 14.0 or higher

• iPhone 11 or newer

• 8GB free storage space
